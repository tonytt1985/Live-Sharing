Clicking on "Help" opens and closes this panel

Video Tool Settings

• Select & Confirm appropriate Webcam and Microphone devices in the video display window to ready for video chat

Queue List

• Only populates when a customer clicks on "talk to us"

• Customers are placed in queue in order of wait time with No.1 being priority

• Hover over queue list and click to manually engage customers

Current Session Data

• Session ID: Auto Generated Number

• Basic Information: Fields for Data entry

• GeoIP Information: Tells you where the customer is calling from

• SAVE: Button click to save/update information on database

Engaging a Customer

• Hover over queue list and click to manually engage customers

• Click on "Go Live" after engagement to initiate video chat with customer

Ending a Session

• Clicking on "End Session" stops and ends the current engagement with the Customer

• You can still edit and save the Current Session Data before your next engagement with a Customer

Disconnection

• The app will attempt to reconnect to a customer in the event of a disconnection within a specified time period

• Clicking the "End Session" button cancels the reconnection
